8Planets

8Planets is a simple viewer and simulator for planetary orbits in our Solar System. It helps you discover how the planets aligned and move around over time so that you feel the scale of the Solar System or even the Universe. The apps are available on Mac, iPhone, iPad, and Apple Watch.
